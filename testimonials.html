Hip Resurfacing

Dan's Story

Hip Resurfacing — The Woodlands Suite, Royal Orthopaedic Hospital

Dan, 43, had been living with severe osteoarthritis for over two years. The pain in his left hip had taken over his daily life — disrupting his sleep, limiting his work, and putting him at risk of ill-health retirement from a career he loved.

After consulting Mr Hussain, Dan chose hip resurfacing. By seven weeks post-surgery he was walking without crutches and sleeping through the night. He is now back playing guitar in his church worship team — something he had not been able to do for over a year.

"The difference you feel is honestly life-changing because you're not living with the underlying pain anymore."
